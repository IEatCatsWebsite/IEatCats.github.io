6 Hours Of Fallout

Cat-eaters, yesterday I spent 6 entire hours playing Fallout: New Vegas. 6 hours of watching a screen change color when i press buttons, and simulations of drug and alchohol addiction, pillaging and murder, drinking 24 sunset sasparillas faster than the speed of light (literally), killing Powergangers and hearing a man talk about his spurs on the radio. That night, I had the best slumber in my life. I had felt as if I had also gone through the Mojave at that point and I finally got to sleep. I'm still not sure if this was a good thing or bad thing because on one hand I played a game for 6 hours, but on the other hand it was Fallout and I had the best sleep of my life.
